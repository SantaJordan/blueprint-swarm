About

Blueprint GTM Methodology

Blueprint GTM is a data-driven approach to go-to-market strategy that starts with what customers actually say, not what we assume they care about.

The core principle is pain-qualified segmentation: instead of targeting companies by size or industry, Blueprint identifies the specific pains that predict buying behavior. Every recommendation in this report is grounded in verbatim evidence from real conversations.

Blueprint Swarm extends this methodology with agentic analysis. A swarm of specialized AI agents processes call transcripts in parallel, each focused on a specific intelligence domain. An audit layer validates every extraction against the source material. The result is comprehensive intelligence that would take a human team weeks, delivered in minutes.

Jordan Crawford
